What We Stand For

From Dialogue to Impact

IPPAD goes beyond talk — translating shared faith values into practical, measurable interventions across Nigeria's communities.

Conflict Prevention & Peace

Joint interfaith peace committees, rapid-response mediation teams, and early warning systems to prevent religious and communal conflict before it escalates.

Trauma Healing & Cohesion

Trauma healing workshops, psychosocial support, and counselling for conflict-affected communities — promoting forgiveness, reconciliation, and emotional healing.

Community Development

Pooling resources to provide basic amenities, humanitarian aid, health advocacy, education, and economic empowerment for vulnerable populations.

"While religion has the power to unite, it is often left unstructured. Yet at their core, Islam and Christianity both uphold shared values — human dignity, justice, compassion, and peace."
— Bishop Dr. Sam Moses Tukura, JP  ·  Founder / Executive Director, IPPAD
Our Programs

Built on Shared Values

Seven pillars of action driving real change in communities across Nigeria.

Advocacy, Justice & Rights

Legal awareness, inclusive governance advocacy, and countering hate speech through faith-based messaging and moral leadership.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Interfaith youth peace clubs, leadership camps, and active participation of women in peacebuilding — preventing radicalization through engagement.

Education & Character

Scholarships for disadvantaged children, support for IDPs and out-of-school youth, and school peace clubs for interfaith engagement.
